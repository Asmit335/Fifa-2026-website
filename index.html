Overview

The FIFA World Cup 2026 will be jointly hosted by the United States, Canada, and Mexico. It will be the first World Cup to feature 48 national teams, making it the biggest tournament in FIFA history.

Format

The expanded tournament features 48 teams divided into 12 groups. The top two teams from each group and the eight best third-placed teams advance to the Round of 32 before progressing through the knockout rounds to the Final.
