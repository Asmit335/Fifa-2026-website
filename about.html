About FIFA World Cup 2026

The FIFA World Cup 2026 is the biggest tournament in football history, bringing together 48 national teams from around the world. Hosted jointly by the United States, Canada, and Mexico, the tournament celebrates the passion, diversity, and unity that football inspires across every continent.

It provides information about the FIFA World Cup 2026, including the tournament overview, participating groups, host cities, memorable champions, match highlights, and an image gallery. The project demonstrates responsive layouts, modern web design, and semantic HTML without using JavaScript.

Our Mission

Our goal is to design a clean, responsive, and user-friendly website that presents FIFA World Cup 2026 information in an attractive and organized way. This project also reflects our learning and practical skills in HTML and CSS while following modern web design principles.
